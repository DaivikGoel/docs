## What is Shor?

Shor is a decentralized payment platform that enables merchants to accept cryptocurrency payments seamlessly. With Shor, you can create payment links, accept USDC payments, and manage transactions - all without complex crypto knowledge.

### Key Features

- **Simple Payment Links**: Create shareable payment links in seconds
- **USDC Support**: Accept stable cryptocurrency payments
- **Smart Contract Security**: Automated, trustless payment distribution
- **Low Fees**: Transparent and competitive pricing
- **Real-time Tracking**: Monitor all your transactions in one dashboard
