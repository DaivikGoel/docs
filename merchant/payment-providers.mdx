## How Providers Work

### Payment Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer Selection">
    Customer chooses their preferred payment method on your payment link
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provider Routing">
    Shor routes to the appropriate payment provider based on:
    - Payment method selected
    - Amount
    - Geographic location
    - Availability
  </Step>
  <Step title="Traditional Payment">
    Customer completes payment using their familiar method:
    - Zelle transfer
    - Venmo payment
    - Bank transfer
  </Step>
  <Step title="Conversion">
    Provider converts traditional payment to USDC:
    - Receives fiat payment
    - Purchases USDC
    - Sends to smart contract
  </Step>
  <Step title="Settlement">
    USDC distributed to your wallet via smart contract
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Provider Selection Logic

### Automatic Routing

Shor automatically selects the best provider based on:

1. **Payment Method**: Customer's preference
2. **Amount**: Within provider limits
3. **Location**: Geographic availability
4. **Performance**: Current success rates
5. **Cost**: Lowest fees for merchant

### Manual Override

In some cases, you can specify preferred providers:
```json
{
  "preferred_providers": ["phoenix", "venmo"],
  "fallback_provider": "wise",
  "block_providers": ["paypal"]
}
```

## Handling Provider Issues

### Common Scenarios

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Payment Delays">
    **Causes**: Bank processing, high volume
    **Solution**: Wait 10-15 minutes, check status
    **Support**: Contact if over 30 minutes
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Failed Matches">
    **Causes**: Wrong memo, amount mismatch
    **Solution**: Manual reconciliation
    **Prevention**: Clear customer instructions
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Provider Downtime">
    **Frequency**: Very rare (99.9% uptime)
    **Backup**: Automatic failover to alternate
    **Communication**: Status page updates
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
