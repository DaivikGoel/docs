## Step-by-Step Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    1. Visit [app.shorpay.com](https://app.shorpay.com)
    2. Click "Sign Up" or "Get Started"
    3. Complete the registration with your email
    4. Verify your email address
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect Your Wallet">
    1. Click "Connect Wallet" in the dashboard
    2. Select MetaMask (or your preferred wallet)
    3. Approve the connection request
    4. Ensure you're on the Base network
    
    <Warning>
    Make sure you're connected to Base network, not Ethereum mainnet. Your wallet should show "Base" in the network selector.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Your Profile">
    Navigate to Settings in your dashboard and set up:
    - Business name
    - Contact information
    - Default payment settings
    - Webhook URLs (optional)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Your First Payment Link">
    1. Click "Create Payment Link" on the dashboard
    2. Fill in the payment details:
       - **Title**: What you're selling
       - **Amount**: Fixed price or customer-specified
       - **Description**: Additional details
       - **Image**: Product or service image (optional)
    3. Choose fee handling (absorb or pass to customer)
    4. Click "Create Link"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Share Your Payment Link">
    Once created, you can:
    - Copy the link to share directly
    - Generate a QR code
    - Send via email or SMS
    - Embed on your website
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Your First Transaction

When a customer completes a payment:

1. **Notification**: You'll see the transaction appear in your dashboard immediately
2. **Settlement**: USDC is sent directly to your wallet (usually within minutes)
3. **Confirmation**: Transaction hash is available for blockchain verification
4. **Status Updates**: Track payment status from pending to completed

## Smart Contract Setup

<Info>
Shor automatically deploys a PayoutSplitter smart contract for you. This contract handles the distribution of funds and ensures secure, automated payments.
</Info>

### What This Means
- Your funds are sent to a smart contract address
- The contract automatically forwards funds to your wallet
- You can configure multiple recipients and percentage splits
- All transactions are transparent and verifiable on-chain

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Security Tips">
    - Never share your wallet's private key
    - Always verify the network (Base) before transactions
    - Use a hardware wallet for large volumes
    - Enable 2FA on your Shor account
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Payment Link Tips">
    - Use descriptive titles and clear amounts
    - Add product images to increase trust
    - Test with a small amount first
    - Save frequently used links as templates
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Customer Communication">
    - Explain that payments settle in USDC
    - Provide clear instructions with the link
    - Include your business name in the description
    - Set up email notifications for confirmations
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Common Questions

<FAQ>
  <FAQItem question="How quickly do I receive payments?">
    Payments typically settle within 2-5 minutes after customer confirmation. The exact time depends on blockchain congestion.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="What are the fees?">
    Shor charges a competitive platform fee. You can see current rates in your dashboard settings and choose whether to absorb or pass fees to customers.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="Can I accept other cryptocurrencies?">
    Currently, all payments settle in USDC on Base network. This provides stability and predictability for your business.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="What if my customer doesn't have crypto?">
    That's the beauty of Shor! Customers can pay with traditional methods like Zelle, and our payment providers handle the conversion to USDC.
  </FAQItem>
</FAQ>
