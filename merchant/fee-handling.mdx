## Fee Overview

Shor charges competitive platform fees to process payments. As a merchant, you have full control over how these fees are handled - either absorb them yourself or pass them to customers.

## Fee Structure

### Platform Fees

Current fee structure:
- **Standard Rate**: 6% of transaction amount
- **No minimum fee**: Percentage-based only
- **No monthly fees**: Pay only for what you use
- **No setup fees**: Start accepting payments immediately

<Info>
Volume discounts available for high-volume merchants. Contact sales@shorpay.com for custom pricing.
</Info>

## Fee Handling Options

### Coming Soon: Customer Pays Fees

<Note>
The ability for customers to pay processing fees is coming soon. This feature will allow you to pass fees to customers transparently.
</Note>

<Tabs>

    - You receive: Full amount

    ```
    Platform Fee: $6.38 (6%)

    ```

  <Tab title="Best For">

    - B2B transactions

  </Tab>

## Choosing Your Fee Strategy

<Info>
Currently, merchants absorb all fees. The option to pass fees to customers is coming soon.
</Info>

### When to Absorb Fees (Current Option)

✅ **Absorb fees when:**
- Competing on price
- Selling to consumers
- Marketing "no hidden fees"
- Products with high margins
- Building trust

❌ **Don't absorb when:**
- Margins are tight
- B2B transactions
- Custom services
- Industry standard is to pass fees

### When to Pass Fees (Coming Soon)

Once available, consider passing fees when:

✅ **Pass fees when:**
- Industry standard practice
- B2B transactions
- Service-based business
- Custom or negotiated pricing
- Preserving margins

❌ **Don't pass when:**
- Competing on price
- Consumer products
- Impulse purchases
- Building market share

<Note>
Shor currently supports USD/USDC transactions. Multi-currency support coming soon.
</Note>

  </FAQItem>
  <FAQItem question="Are there hidden fees?">
    No. The platform fee covers everything .
  </FAQItem>
