## Transaction Analytics

### Transaction Volume

Monitor transaction patterns:
- **Hourly Distribution**: Peak transaction times
- **Day of Week**: Busiest days
- **Success Rate**: Completed vs failed transactions
- **Processing Time**: Average settlement speed

### Transaction Details

| Metric | Description | Use Case |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **Success Rate** | % of completed transactions | Identify issues |
| **Avg Processing** | Time from initiation to completion | Customer experience |
| **Failure Reasons** | Why transactions fail | Process improvement |
| **Retry Rate** | Customers who try again | User experience |

## Financial Analytics

### Fee Analysis

Understand fee impact:
- **Total Fees Paid**: Platform fees over time
- **Fee Percentage**: Fees as % of revenue
- **Fee Strategy Impact**: Absorb vs pass comparison
- **Optimization Opportunities**: Ways to reduce fee impact

### Cash Flow Insights

<Info>
All payments settle in USDC within minutes, providing predictable cash flow unlike traditional payment processors.
</Info>

Monitor:
- **Daily Settlement**: USDC received each day
- **Pending Payments**: In-progress transactions
- **Settlement Speed**: Average time to receive funds
- **Wallet Balance**: Current USDC holdings
