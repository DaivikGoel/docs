## Browser Extension Setup

### What You'll Need
- A computer with internet access
- A web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, or Edge)
- A secure place to store your backup information

### Step 1: Install MetaMask Extension

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="download" title="Download MetaMask Extension">
    1. Go to the official MetaMask website: [metamask.io](https://metamask.io)
    2. Click the "Download" button
    3. Choose your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, or Edge)
    4. Click "Install MetaMask for [Your Browser]"
    5. Follow your browser's installation prompts
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="shield" title="Verify Installation">
    After installation, you should see:
    - A fox icon in your browser's extension area
    - A welcome screen when you click the MetaMask icon
    - The MetaMask extension asking for permission to run
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Create Your Wallet

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="plus" title="Start Wallet Creation">
    1. Click the MetaMask fox icon in your browser
    2. Click "Get Started"
    3. Choose "Create a Wallet" (not "Import Wallet")
    4. Read and accept the terms of service
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="key" title="Set Up Your Password">
    1. Create a strong password (8+ characters, mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
    2. This password protects your wallet on this device
    3. Write down your password in a secure location
    4. Click "Create"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="seedling" title="Backup Your Seed Phrase">
    **This is the most important step!**
    
    1. MetaMask will show you 12 random words (your "seed phrase")
    2. Click "Reveal Secret Words" to see them
    3. Write down all 12 words in order on paper
    4. Store this paper in a safe, secure location
    5. Never share these words with anyone
    6. Click "Next" after writing them down
    7. Confirm by selecting the words in the correct order
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Browser Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="lock" title="Password Protection">
    - Use a strong, unique password
    - Never save your password in browser autocomplete
    - Consider using a password manager
    - Lock MetaMask when not in use (click profile icon → Lock)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="shield" title="Browser Security Tips">
    - Only use MetaMask on trusted devices
    - Be cautious with browser extensions
    - Clear browser cache if switching devices
    - Use a dedicated browser profile for crypto
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="plug" title="Extension Management">
    - Pin MetaMask to your browser toolbar
    - Check for official updates regularly
    - Never install MetaMask from unofficial sources
    - Be wary of fake MetaMask extensions
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Mobile App Setup

### What You'll Need
- An iOS or Android smartphone
- A secure place to store your backup information
- The official MetaMask app (not a clone)

### Step 1: Download MetaMask Mobile

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="apple" title="iOS Installation">
    1. Open the App Store
    2. Search for "MetaMask - Blockchain Wallet"
    3. Verify the developer is "MetaMask"
    4. Tap "Get" to download
    5. Wait for installation to complete
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="android" title="Android Installation">
    1. Open Google Play Store
    2. Search for "MetaMask - Crypto Wallet"
    3. Verify the developer is "MetaMask"
    4. Tap "Install"
    5. Wait for installation to complete
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Create Your Mobile Wallet

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="plus" title="Start Wallet Creation">
    1. Open the MetaMask app
    2. Tap "Get started"
    3. Choose "Create a new wallet"
    4. Review and accept the terms
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="key" title="Set Up Security">
    1. Create a strong password
    2. Enable biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID/Fingerprint)
    3. Watch the security video (required)
    4. Tap "Continue" when ready
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="seedling" title="Backup Your Seed Phrase">
    **Critical Step - Do Not Skip!**
    
    1. Tap "Start" to reveal your seed phrase
    2. Write down all 12 words in order on paper
    3. Store this paper in a secure location
    4. Never take a screenshot or photo
    5. Tap "Continue" after securing your phrase
    6. Confirm by selecting words in the correct order
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Mobile-Specific Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="sync" title="Sync with Browser Extension">
    If you want to use the same wallet on desktop:
    
    1. Install MetaMask browser extension first
    2. In browser: Settings → Advanced → Sync with Mobile
    3. In mobile app: Settings → Sync with Extension
    4. Scan the QR code with your phone
    5. Your accounts sync automatically
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="globe" title="Connect to dApps">
    1. Open the built-in browser (bottom menu)
    2. Navigate to any dApp website
    3. Click "Connect Wallet" on the site
    4. Approve the connection request
    
    Or use WalletConnect:
    1. Look for WalletConnect option on desktop dApps
    2. Scan the QR code with MetaMask mobile
    3. Approve the connection
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="shield" title="Mobile Security Settings">
    1. Go to Settings → Security & Privacy
    2. Enable "Auto-Lock" (recommended: 5 minutes)
    3. Turn on "Require Authentication" for transactions
    4. Consider enabling "Privacy Mode"
    5. Disable "Join MetaMask Beta" unless testing
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="shield-alt" title="Mobile-Specific Security">
    - **Public WiFi**: Avoid transactions on public networks
    - **App Permissions**: Only grant necessary permissions
    - **Screenshots**: Never screenshot your seed phrase
    - **Auto-lock**: Set a short timer (5 minutes recommended)
    - **App Updates**: Keep MetaMask updated through official app stores
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Using Your MetaMask Wallet

The following sections apply to both browser extension and mobile app users.

### Understanding Your Wallet

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Account Address" icon="address-book">
    Your wallet has a unique address (like a bank account number) that starts with 0x. This is what you share to receive crypto.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Private Key" icon="key">
    A secret code that proves you own your wallet. Never share this with anyone.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Seed Phrase" icon="seedling">
    The 12 words that can restore your entire wallet. Keep these safe and private.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Network" icon="globe">
    MetaMask can connect to different blockchain networks. Ethereum is the default.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="eye" title="View Your Wallet">
    **Browser:**
    1. Click the MetaMask fox icon
    2. View your account address (click to copy)
    3. Check your balance (0 ETH initially)
    
    **Mobile:**
    1. Open the MetaMask app
    2. Your address appears at the top
    3. Tap to copy your address
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="network-wired" title="Understanding Networks">
    MetaMask can connect to different blockchain networks:
    
    - **Base Mainnet**: The Base network where Shor processes USDC payments (real money)
    - **Ethereum Mainnet**: The main Ethereum network 
    - **Testnets**: Practice networks (fake money for testing)
    - **Other Networks**: Polygon, BSC, Arbitrum, etc.
    
    For Shor payments, you'll need to use Base Mainnet.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="wallet" title="Getting Your First Crypto">
    To start using MetaMask, you'll need cryptocurrency:
    
    1. **Buy from an exchange** (Coinbase, Kraken, Binance)
    2. **Receive from someone** (share your wallet address)
    3. **Purchase directly in MetaMask** (via MoonPay, Transak)
    4. **Buy directly from Shor** when receiving your first payment
    
    Note: You need ETH to pay for transaction fees on Ethereum.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Security Best Practices

<Warning>
**Never share your private keys or seed phrase with anyone, including:**
- Customer support teams
- Websites asking for your keys
- People claiming to be MetaMask support
- Anyone promising to help you recover funds
</Warning>

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Secure Storage" icon="vault">
    Store your seed phrase on paper in a fireproof safe or safety deposit box. Never store it digitally.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Strong Passwords" icon="lock">
    Use a unique password for MetaMask that you don't use elsewhere. Consider a password manager.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Device Security" icon="shield">
    Only use MetaMask on devices you trust and control. Avoid public computers.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Regular Backups" icon="copy">
    Keep multiple copies of your seed phrase in different secure locations.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Common Actions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="arrow-right" title="Sending Crypto">
    1. Click "Send" in MetaMask
    2. Enter the recipient's address
    3. Choose the amount to send
    4. Review the transaction fee (gas fee)
    5. Click "Confirm" to send
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="arrow-left" title="Receiving Crypto">
    1. Click "Account" to copy your address
    2. Share this address with the sender
    3. Wait for the transaction to confirm
    4. Your balance will update automatically
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="gear" title="Managing Settings">
    - **Account Details**: View your address and export private key
    - **Security**: Change password, lock/unlock wallet
    - **Networks**: Add or switch between blockchain networks
    - **Advanced**: Reset account, clear activity
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="question" title="MetaMask Not Loading">
    - Refresh your browser
    - Check if the extension is enabled
    - Try restarting your browser
    - Reinstall the extension if needed
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="exclamation-triangle" title="Transaction Failed">
    - Check if you have enough ETH for gas fees
    - Verify the recipient address is correct
    - Try increasing the gas limit
    - Check network congestion
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="key" title="Forgot Password">
    - Use your seed phrase to restore your wallet
    - Click "Import Wallet" in MetaMask
    - Enter your 12-word seed phrase
    - Set a new password
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="seedling" title="Lost Seed Phrase">
    **Warning**: If you lose your seed phrase, you cannot recover your wallet or funds.
    
    - Check all your secure storage locations
    - Look for any written copies you may have made
    - If truly lost, you'll need to create a new wallet
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
