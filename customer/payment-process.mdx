## Payment Overview

This guide provides a detailed walkthrough of the entire payment process, from receiving a link to getting your confirmation.

## Before You Start

### What You'll Need

<Checklist>
- [ ] Payment link from merchant
- [ ] Access to Zelle through your bank
- [ ] Email address for receipt
- [ ] Bank account with Zelle enabled
</Checklist>

### Quick Checks

Before starting payment:
1. **Verify Merchant**: Ensure you recognize the seller
2. **Check Amount**: Confirm price is correct
3. **Read Description**: Understand what you're paying for
4. **Note Fees**: See if fees are included

## Detailed Payment Steps

### Step 1: Open Payment Link

When you click the payment link:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/payment-link-example.png" alt="Payment Link Page" />
</Frame>

You'll see:
- **Merchant Logo/Image**: Visual identifier
- **Title**: What you're purchasing
- **Amount**: How much to pay
- **Description**: Additional details
- **Pay Button**: Start payment process

<Tip>
Screenshot or bookmark the page for reference during payment.
</Tip>

### Step 2: Enter Your Information

Click "Pay Now" and provide:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Required Info">
    **Email Address**:
    - For payment confirmation
    - Receipt delivery
    - Support contact
    
    Enter carefully - this is how you'll receive confirmation!
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Optional Info">
    Some merchants may request:
    - Name
    - Phone number
    - Delivery address
    - Order notes
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Step 3: Phoenix Payment Window Opens

After clicking "Pay Now":

<Info>
Payments are processed through Phoenix Market, which offers two convenient Zelle payment options.
</Info>

You'll see the Phoenix payment interface with two options:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Automated Zelle" icon="bolt">
    - Connect bank via Teller
    - Automatic Zelle payment
    - Faster processing
    - No manual steps
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manual Zelle" icon="hand">
    - Use your bank's Zelle
    - Send manually
    - Traditional method
    - Works with any Zelle bank
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Step 4: Choose Your Zelle Method

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Option A: Automated Zelle">
    If you choose automated Zelle:
    
    1. **Click "Connect Bank"** in Phoenix interface
    2. **Select your bank** from Teller's list
    3. **Log in securely** to your online banking
    4. **Authorize connection** for this payment only
    5. **Confirm Zelle payment** details
    
    <Warning>
    Your banking credentials are encrypted and never shared with merchants. Teller provides bank-level security.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Option B: Manual Zelle">
    If you choose manual Zelle:
    
    1. **Receive recipient details** from Phoenix
    2. **Open your banking app** or Zelle app
    3. **Add recipient** using provided email/phone
    4. **Send exact amount** shown
    5. **Return to Phoenix** and confirm sent
    
    <Note>
    No reference codes needed - Phoenix automatically matches your payment.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Step 5: Complete Zelle Payment

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Automated Zelle Process">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select Account">
        Choose which account to send Zelle from
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Review Details">
        - Verify payment amount
        - Check recipient info
        - Confirm total to send
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Authorize Payment">
        - Zelle sent automatically
        - No manual steps needed
        - Instant processing begins
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Conversion">
        - Phoenix receives Zelle
        - Converts USD to USDC
        - Sends to merchant wallet
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Manual Zelle Process">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Send via Your Bank">
        - Open your banking app
        - Go to Zelle section
        - Send to provided recipient
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Enter Exact Amount">
        - Match amount exactly
        - Include cents if shown
        - No extra fees
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Complete & Return">
        - Send the Zelle payment
        - Return to Phoenix window
        - Click "I've sent payment"
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Verification">
        - Phoenix verifies receipt
        - Converts to USDC
        - Completes transaction
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Step 6: Payment Processing

After authorizing the transfer:

1. Stay on the payment page
2. Phoenix processes your payment automatically
3. You'll see real-time status updates

The payment window shows:
```
Verifying Zelle payment...
Converting USD to USDC
Sending to merchant wallet
Transaction complete!
```

### Step 7: Payment Confirmation

Once processed, you'll see:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Success Screen">
    ✅ **Payment Successful!**
    
    - Transaction ID
    - Amount paid
    - Timestamp
    - Merchant details
    - "View Receipt" button
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email Receipt">
    Check your email for:
    - Payment confirmation
    - Transaction details
    - Blockchain verification
    - Support contact
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### What Happens Behind the Scenes

  <TimelineItem title="Payment Method Selected" time="0:00">

  </TimelineItem>
  <TimelineItem title="Zelle Payment Sent" time="0:30">

  </TimelineItem>
  <TimelineItem title="Phoenix Receives Payment" time="1:00">
    Phoenix verifies Zelle receipt
  </TimelineItem>
  <TimelineItem title="USD to USDC Conversion" time="2:00">
    Phoenix converts your USD to USDC
  </TimelineItem>
  <TimelineItem title="Blockchain Settlement" time="3:00">
    USDC sent to merchant's wallet on Base
  </TimelineItem>
  <TimelineItem title="Confirmation" time="3:30">
    All parties notified of successful payment
  </TimelineItem>

### How Payments Are Tracked

Your payment is automatically tracked through:
- **Order ID**: Created when you initiate payment
- **Zelle Transaction**: Matched by Phoenix automatically
- **Blockchain Transaction**: Verifiable on Base network
- **Email Confirmation**: Sent to your provided email

<Info>
Phoenix automatically matches your Zelle payment - no reference codes needed!
</Info>

## Common Scenarios

### Fixed Amount Payments

Most common type:
- Merchant sets exact price
- You pay the amount shown
- No guesswork involved

### Variable Amount Payments

For donations or custom amounts:
- You enter desired amount
- Minimum may apply
- Confirm before sending

## Payment Tips

### For Smooth Transactions

✅ **Do:**
- Use a secure internet connection
- Have Zelle access ready
- Send exact amount shown
- Complete the process in one session
- Save confirmations
- Check email for receipt

❌ **Don't:**
- Share your bank credentials
- Use public WiFi for payments
- Refresh during processing
- Close the payment window early
- Make multiple payment attempts

- Payment screenshot
