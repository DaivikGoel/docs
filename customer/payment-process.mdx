## Payment Overview

This guide provides a detailed walkthrough of the entire payment process, from receiving a link to getting your confirmation.

## Before You Start

### What You'll Need

<Checklist>
- [ ] Payment link from merchant
- [ ] Access to your online banking (for account connection)
- [ ] Email address for receipt
- [ ] Bank account with sufficient funds
</Checklist>

### Quick Checks

Before starting payment:
1. **Verify Merchant**: Ensure you recognize the seller
2. **Check Amount**: Confirm price is correct
3. **Read Description**: Understand what you're paying for
4. **Note Fees**: See if fees are included

## Detailed Payment Steps

### Step 1: Open Payment Link

When you click the payment link:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/payment-link-example.png" alt="Payment Link Page" />
</Frame>

You'll see:
- **Merchant Logo/Image**: Visual identifier
- **Title**: What you're purchasing
- **Amount**: How much to pay
- **Description**: Additional details
- **Pay Button**: Start payment process

<Tip>
Screenshot or bookmark the page for reference during payment.
</Tip>

### Step 2: Enter Your Information

Click "Pay Now" and provide:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Required Info">
    **Email Address**:
    - For payment confirmation
    - Receipt delivery
    - Support contact
    
    Enter carefully - this is how you'll receive confirmation!
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Optional Info">
    Some merchants may request:
    - Name
    - Phone number
    - Delivery address
    - Order notes
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Step 3: Select Payment Method

Choose your payment method:

<Card title="Bank Transfer (ACH)" icon="building-columns">
  - Secure bank account connection via Teller
  - Supported by most US banks
  - Automatic transfer from your account
  - Processes within minutes
</Card>

<Info>
Payments are processed through our secure payment partner Phoenix, which handles the conversion from USD to USDC.
</Info>

### Step 4: Connect Your Bank Account

After clicking "Pay Now", you'll see a secure payment window:

1. **Phoenix Payment Interface** opens in a secure frame
2. **Connect Bank Account** button appears
3. **Teller Integration** allows you to securely link your bank
4. **Select your bank** from the list of supported institutions
5. **Log in securely** using your online banking credentials

<Warning>
Your banking credentials are never shared with Shor or the merchant. Teller uses bank-level encryption and security.
</Warning>

### Step 5: Complete Bank Transfer

Once your bank is connected:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Account">
    Choose which bank account to pay from
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Amount">
    - Verify the payment amount
    - Check any applicable fees
    - See total to be debited
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Transfer">
    - Review transfer details
    - Confirm the payment
    - May require additional bank verification
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Processing">
    - Phoenix initiates the ACH transfer
    - Converts USD to USDC
    - Sends to merchant's wallet
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 6: Payment Processing

After authorizing the transfer:

1. Stay on the payment page
2. Phoenix processes your payment automatically
3. You'll see real-time status updates

The payment window shows:
```
Processing your payment...
Converting USD to USDC
Sending to merchant wallet
```

### Step 7: Payment Confirmation

Once processed, you'll see:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Success Screen">
    ✅ **Payment Successful!**
    
    - Transaction ID
    - Amount paid
    - Timestamp
    - Merchant details
    - "View Receipt" button
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email Receipt">
    Check your email for:
    - Payment confirmation
    - Transaction details
    - Blockchain verification
    - Support contact
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Understanding the Process

### What Happens Behind the Scenes

<Timeline>
  <TimelineItem title="Bank Account Connected" time="0:00">
    You securely connect your bank through Teller
  </TimelineItem>
  <TimelineItem title="ACH Transfer Initiated" time="0:30">
    Phoenix initiates transfer from your bank account
  </TimelineItem>
  <TimelineItem title="USD to USDC Conversion" time="1:00">
    Phoenix converts your USD payment to USDC
  </TimelineItem>
  <TimelineItem title="Blockchain Transaction" time="2:00">
    USDC sent to merchant's wallet on Base network
  </TimelineItem>
  <TimelineItem title="Confirmation" time="3:00">
    All parties notified of successful payment
  </TimelineItem>
</Timeline>

### How Payments Are Tracked

Your payment is automatically tracked through:
- **Order ID**: Created when you initiate payment
- **Bank Connection**: Linked to your specific transaction
- **Blockchain Transaction**: Verifiable on Base network
- **Email Confirmation**: Sent to your provided email

<Info>
All payments are automatically matched and processed - no manual reference codes needed!
</Info>

## Common Scenarios

### Fixed Amount Payments

Most common type:
- Merchant sets exact price
- You pay the amount shown
- No guesswork involved

### Variable Amount Payments

For donations or custom amounts:
- You enter desired amount
- Minimum may apply
- Confirm before sending

## Payment Tips

### For Smooth Transactions

✅ **Do:**
- Use a secure internet connection
- Have your bank login ready
- Complete the process in one session
- Save confirmations
- Check email for receipt

❌ **Don't:**
- Share your bank credentials
- Use public WiFi for payments
- Refresh during processing
- Close the payment window early
- Make multiple payment attempts
