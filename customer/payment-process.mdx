## Payment Overview

This guide provides a detailed walkthrough of the entire payment process, from receiving a link to getting your confirmation.

## Before You Start

### What You'll Need

<Checklist>
- [ ] Payment link from merchant
- [ ] Access to your banking app (with Zelle)
- [ ] Email address for receipt
- [ ] Exact payment amount
</Checklist>

### Quick Checks

Before starting payment:
1. **Verify Merchant**: Ensure you recognize the seller
2. **Check Amount**: Confirm price is correct
3. **Read Description**: Understand what you're paying for
4. **Note Fees**: See if fees are included

## Detailed Payment Steps

### Step 1: Open Payment Link

When you click the payment link:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/payment-link-example.png" alt="Payment Link Page" />
</Frame>

You'll see:
- **Merchant Logo/Image**: Visual identifier
- **Title**: What you're purchasing
- **Amount**: How much to pay
- **Description**: Additional details
- **Pay Button**: Start payment process

<Tip>
Screenshot or bookmark the page for reference during payment.
</Tip>

### Step 2: Enter Your Information

Click "Pay Now" and provide:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Required Info">
    **Email Address**:
    - For payment confirmation
    - Receipt delivery
    - Support contact
    
    Enter carefully - this is how you'll receive confirmation!
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Optional Info">
    Some merchants may request:
    - Name
    - Phone number
    - Delivery address
    - Order notes
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Step 3: Select Payment Method

Choose your payment method:

<Card title="Zelle (Available Now)" icon="building-columns">
  - Instant bank transfers
  - Supported by major US banks
  - No additional Zelle fees
  - Processes in 2-5 minutes
</Card>

<Info>
More payment methods coming soon! Currently, Zelle is the primary option for US customers.
</Info>

### Step 4: Payment Instructions

After selecting Zelle, you'll receive:

```
Pay via Zelle:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Recipient: payments@shorpay.com
Amount: $125.00 (exact amount required)
Reference: SHR-A7B9-X2M4

IMPORTANT: Include reference in memo!
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
```

<Warning>
Copy these details exactly. The reference code is crucial for matching your payment.
</Warning>

### Step 5: Make Zelle Payment

Open your banking app:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Zelle">
    Look for "Send with Zelle" or Zelle icon in your banking app
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Recipient">
    - Enter the exact email provided
    - May show as "Shor Payments" or similar
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Amount">
    - Type the EXACT amount shown
    - Don't round up or down
    - Include cents (e.g., $125.00)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Reference">
    - Find memo/note field
    - Enter the reference code
    - Double-check accuracy
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send Payment">
    - Review all details
    - Confirm and send
    - Note confirmation number
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 6: Confirm on Shor

Return to the Shor payment page:

1. Click "I've Completed Payment"
2. Wait for processing (usually 2-5 minutes)
3. Don't close the page yet!

You'll see a processing indicator:
```
Processing your payment...
This usually takes 2-5 minutes
Please don't close this page
```

### Step 7: Payment Confirmation

Once processed, you'll see:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Success Screen">
    ✅ **Payment Successful!**
    
    - Transaction ID
    - Amount paid
    - Timestamp
    - Merchant details
    - "View Receipt" button
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email Receipt">
    Check your email for:
    - Payment confirmation
    - Transaction details
    - Blockchain verification
    - Support contact
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Understanding the Process

### What Happens Behind the Scenes

<Timeline>
  <TimelineItem title="You Send Zelle Payment" time="0:00">
    Payment sent from your bank to Shor's payment processor
  </TimelineItem>
  <TimelineItem title="Payment Detected" time="0:30">
    System matches your payment using reference code
  </TimelineItem>
  <TimelineItem title="Conversion Initiated" time="1:00">
    Your USD payment is converted to USDC cryptocurrency
  </TimelineItem>
  <TimelineItem title="Blockchain Transaction" time="2:00">
    USDC sent to merchant's smart contract
  </TimelineItem>
  <TimelineItem title="Confirmation" time="3:00">
    All parties notified of successful payment
  </TimelineItem>
</Timeline>

### Why Reference Codes Matter

The reference code (e.g., SHR-A7B9-X2M4) is critical because:
- Links your payment to your order
- Ensures accurate delivery
- Enables automatic processing
- Prevents payment mix-ups

<Warning>
Without the correct reference code, your payment may be delayed or require manual intervention.
</Warning>

## Common Scenarios

### Fixed Amount Payments

Most common type:
- Merchant sets exact price
- You pay the amount shown
- No guesswork involved

### Variable Amount Payments

For donations or custom amounts:
- You enter desired amount
- Minimum may apply
- Confirm before sending

## Payment Tips

### For Smooth Transactions

✅ **Do:**
- Pay exact amounts
- Include reference codes
- Complete quickly (within 30 min)
- Save confirmations
- Check email for receipt

❌ **Don't:**
- Round payment amounts
- Skip the reference code
- Use multiple payments
- Close page while processing
- Share payment details
