## Available Payment Methods

Shor integrates with payment methods you already know and trust. You don't need cryptocurrency - just use your regular payment apps and we handle the rest.

## Currently Available

### Zelle

<Card icon="building-columns" title="Zelle - Bank Direct Transfer">

#### About Zelle

Zelle is a fast, safe, and easy way to send money directly between bank accounts in the US. It's built into most major banking apps.

#### Supported Banks

Major banks with built-in Zelle:
- Bank of America
- Chase
- Wells Fargo
- Capital One
- US Bank
- PNC Bank
- TD Bank
- Truist
- And 1000+ more

<Info>
If your bank doesn't have Zelle built-in, you can use the standalone Zelle app with a debit card.
</Info>

#### How to Pay with Zelle

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="In Banking App">
    1. Open your banking app
    2. Find "Zelle" or "Send Money with Zelle"
    3. Add recipient email from Shor
    4. Enter exact amount
    5. Add reference code to memo
    6. Send payment
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Standalone Zelle App">
    1. Download Zelle app
    2. Link your debit card
    3. Add recipient email
    4. Enter payment details
    5. Include reference code
    6. Complete payment
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

#### Zelle Payment Tips

‚úÖ **Best Practices:**
- Double-check recipient email
- Match amount exactly (including cents)
- Always include reference code
- Complete within 30 minutes
- Save confirmation screen

‚ùå **Common Mistakes:**
- Rounding payment amounts
- Forgetting reference code
- Using wrong recipient
- Splitting into multiple payments
- Delaying payment completion

## Frequently Asked Questions

<FAQ>
  <FAQItem question="Why is only Zelle available right now?">
    We're starting with Zelle due to its speed, security, and wide US bank integration. More methods are actively being added.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="Will you add cryptocurrency payments directly?">
    We're focused on traditional payment methods for now, but may add direct crypto payments in the future for users who prefer them.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="Can I use multiple payment methods for one purchase?">
    Currently, each transaction must use a single payment method. Split payments aren't supported.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="Are there different fees for different payment methods?">
    No, the Shor platform fee is the same regardless of payment method chosen.
  </FAQItem>
</FAQ>
