## Available Payment Methods

Shor integrates with payment methods you already know and trust. You don't need cryptocurrency - just use your regular payment apps and we handle the rest.

## Currently Available

### Zelle

<Card icon="building-columns" title="Zelle - Bank Direct Transfer">
  - **Status**: ✅ Available Now
  - **Coverage**: United States
  - **Processing Time**: 5 seconds
  - **Limits**: $1 - $1,500 per transaction
</Card>

#### About Zelle

Zelle is a fast, safe, and easy way to send money directly between bank accounts in the US. It's built into most major banking apps.

#### Supported Banks

Major banks with built-in Zelle:
- Bank of America
- Chase
- Wells Fargo
- Capital One
- US Bank
- PNC Bank
- TD Bank
- Truist
- And 1000+ more

<Info>
If your bank doesn't have Zelle built-in, you can use the standalone Zelle app with a debit card.
</Info>

#### How to Pay with Zelle

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="In Banking App">
    1. Open your banking app
    2. Find "Zelle" or "Send Money with Zelle"
    3. Add recipient email from Shor
    4. Enter exact amount
    5. Send payment
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Standalone Zelle App">
    1. Download Zelle app
    2. Link your debit card
    3. Add recipient email
    4. Enter payment details
    5. Complete payment
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

#### Zelle Payment Tips

✅ **Best Practices:**
- Double-check recipient email
- Match amount exactly (including cents)
- Send exact amount as shown
- Complete within 30 minutes
- Save confirmation screen

❌ **Common Mistakes:**
- Rounding payment amounts
- Using wrong email/phone for recipient
- Using wrong recipient
- Splitting into multiple payments
- Delaying payment completion

## Troubleshooting Payment Methods

### Zelle Issues

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Can't Find Recipient">
    - Double-check email address
    - Try phone number if provided
    - Ensure no typos
    - Contact support if persists
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Payment Limit Reached">
    - Check daily/weekly limits
    - Wait for limit reset
    - Try smaller amount
    - Contact your bank
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Payment Pending">
    - New recipients may pend
    - Usually clears in minutes
    - Check bank notifications
    - Verify recipient details
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
