## What is Shor?

Shor is a payment platform that lets you pay merchants using familiar payment methods like Zelle, while they receive cryptocurrency. You don't need to understand or own cryptocurrency - Shor handles all the complexity for you.

## Why Merchants Use Shor

Merchants choose Shor because it offers:
- Instant settlement of payments
- Lower fees than credit cards
- No chargebacks or payment reversals
- Global payment acceptance

### What Happens to Your Payment

1. **You Pay**: Using Zelle or other methods
2. **Provider Receives**: Payment processor gets your funds
3. **Conversion**: Funds converted to USDC (stablecoin)
4. **Merchant Paid**: USDC sent to merchant's wallet
5. **Confirmation**: All parties notified

<Info>
This entire process typically completes in 2-5 minutes!
</Info>
