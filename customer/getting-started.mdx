## Welcome to Shor!

Shor makes it easy to pay merchants using your preferred payment method. This guide will walk you through making your first payment using a Shor payment link.

## What You'll Need

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Zelle Access" icon="building-columns">
    A US bank account with Zelle enabled. Check your banking app for the Zelle feature.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email Address" icon="envelope">
    For receiving payment confirmations and receipts
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How Shor Works for You

When a merchant sends you a Shor payment link, you can pay using Zelle (through Phoenix Market) while the merchant receives cryptocurrency (USDC). You don't need to know anything about crypto!

### Benefits for You

- **Zelle Payments**: Use the payment method you already know
- **Two Options**: Choose automated or manual Zelle
- **Fast Processing**: Payments complete in minutes
- **Clear Confirmations**: Instant receipt of your payment

## Making Your First Payment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Payment Link">
    Open the link sent by the merchant. You'll see:
    - What you're paying for
    - The amount (or option to enter amount)
    - Merchant information
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Email">
    Provide your email address to receive:
    - Payment confirmation
    - Transaction receipt
    - Support if needed
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Payment">
    Click "Pay Now" to begin:
    - Opens Phoenix Market payment window
    - Choose between two Zelle options
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Zelle Method">
    Phoenix offers two ways to pay:
    
    **Automated Zelle** (Recommended):
    - Connect bank via Teller
    - Automatic payment processing
    - Faster confirmation
    
    **Manual Zelle**:
    - Use your own bank app
    - Follow provided instructions
    - Traditional Zelle transfer
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive Confirmation">
    Once processed:
    - See confirmation on screen
    - Receive email receipt
    - Payment is complete!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payment Method: Zelle

### Phoenix Market Integration

<Info>
Phoenix Market processes your Zelle payment and converts it to USDC for the merchant. They offer two convenient ways to send Zelle.
</Info>

#### Automated Zelle (Easier & Faster)

1. **Bank Connection**
   - Connect via Teller's secure API
   - One-time authorization only
   - Bank-level encryption

2. **Automatic Processing**
   - Zelle sent automatically
   - No manual steps needed
   - Instant confirmation

#### Manual Zelle (Traditional)

1. **Get Instructions**
   - Receive recipient details
   - Note exact amount

2. **Send via Your Bank**
   - Use your banking app
   - Send to provided recipient
   - Confirm when sent

### Supported Banks

**For Automated Zelle** (via Teller):
- Bank of America
- Chase
- Wells Fargo
- US Bank
- Capital One
- And many more...

**For Manual Zelle**:
- Any US bank with Zelle

<Note>
If your bank isn't supported by Teller, simply use the Manual Zelle option instead.
</Note>

## Understanding Fees

### Who Pays Fees?

The merchant decides whether to:
- **Absorb fees**: You pay the exact amount shown
- **Pass fees**: You pay the amount plus a small processing fee

## Common Questions

<FAQ>
  <FAQItem question="Do I need cryptocurrency to pay?">
    No! You pay with traditional methods like Zelle. The conversion to crypto happens behind the scenes.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="Is this safe?">
    Yes. Shor uses bank-level security and regulated payment providers. Your banking details are never stored.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="How long does payment take?">
    Automated Zelle: 2-3 minutes. Manual Zelle: 5-10 minutes for verification.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="Which Zelle option should I use?">
    We recommend Automated Zelle for speed and convenience. Use Manual Zelle if your bank isn't supported by Teller or you prefer not to connect your account.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="Is connecting my bank safe?">
    Yes! Automated Zelle uses Teller, a secure banking API. Your credentials are encrypted and never shared with merchants or Shor.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="What if I need help?">
    Contact support@shorpay.com with your transaction details, and we'll assist you promptly.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="Can I get a refund?">
    Refund policies are set by the merchant. Contact them directly for refund requests.
  </FAQItem>
</FAQ>

## Tips for Smooth Payments

### Do's
- ✅ **Secure Connection**: Use a trusted internet connection
- ✅ **Verify Amount**: Check the transfer amount carefully
- ✅ **Complete Process**: Stay on page until confirmation
- ✅ **Save Receipts**: Keep email confirmations

### Don'ts
- ❌ **Public WiFi**: Avoid banking on public networks
- ❌ **Share Credentials**: Never share bank login info
- ❌ **Multiple Attempts**: Don't retry if first attempt is processing
- ❌ **Close Window**: Keep payment window open until complete

## Getting Help

### Before Contacting Support

Check these first:
1. Payment amount matches exactly
2. Payment sent to correct recipient
3. Used correct recipient details
4. Waited 5-10 minutes for processing

### Contact Support

If you need help:
- **Email**: support@shorpay.com
- **Include**: Transaction details, screenshot
- **Response Time**: Usually within 24 hours

### Information to Provide

When contacting support, include:
- Payment link URL
- Transaction amount
- Payment method used
- Time of payment
- Any error messages
