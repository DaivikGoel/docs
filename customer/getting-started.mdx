## Welcome to Shor!

Shor makes it easy to pay merchants using your preferred payment method. This guide will walk you through making your first payment using a Shor payment link.

## How Shor Works for You

When a merchant sends you a Shor payment link, you can pay using familiar methods like Zelle, while the merchant receives cryptocurrency (USDC). You don't need to know anything about crypto!

### Benefits for You

- **Familiar Payment Methods**: Use Zelle just like you normally would
- **Secure Transactions**: Protected by blockchain technology
- **Fast Processing**: Payments complete in minutes
- **Clear Confirmations**: Instant receipt of your payment

## Making Your First Payment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Payment Link">
    Open the link sent by the merchant. You'll see:
    - What you're paying for
    - The amount (or option to enter amount)
    - Merchant information
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Email">
    Provide your email address to receive:
    - Payment confirmation
    - Transaction receipt
    - Support if needed
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Payment Method">
    Select your preferred payment method:
    - **Zelle**: Available now for US customers
    - More options coming soon
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Follow the instructions for your chosen method:
    - You'll receive specific payment details
    - Complete payment in your banking app
    - Return to confirm completion
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive Confirmation">
    Once processed:
    - See confirmation on screen
    - Receive email receipt
    - Payment is complete!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payment Methods

### Zelle Payments

<Info>
Zelle is currently the primary payment method. You'll need a US bank account with Zelle enabled.
</Info>

#### How to Pay with Zelle

1. **Open Your Banking App**
   - Most major US banks support Zelle
   - Look for "Send with Zelle" option

2. **Enter Payment Details**
   - Recipient email/phone (provided by Shor)
   - Exact amount shown
   - Reference code in memo field

3. **Confirm and Send**
   - Double-check amount matches exactly
   - Include the reference code
   - Send payment

4. **Return to Shor**
   - Click "I've completed payment"
   - Wait for confirmation (usually 2-5 minutes)

## Understanding Fees

### Who Pays Fees?

The merchant decides whether to:
- **Absorb fees**: You pay the exact amount shown
- **Pass fees**: You pay the amount plus a small processing fee

<Note>
Fees will always be clearly shown before you complete payment. No hidden charges!
</Note>

### Fee Transparency

Before paying, you'll see:
- Base amount
- Processing fee (if any)
- Total you'll pay

Example:
```
Product: $100.00
Processing Fee: $2.90
Total to Pay: $102.90
```

## Common Questions

<FAQ>
  <FAQItem question="Do I need cryptocurrency to pay?">
    No! You pay with traditional methods like Zelle. The conversion to crypto happens behind the scenes.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="Is this safe?">
    Yes. Shor uses bank-level security and regulated payment providers. Your banking details are never stored.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="How long does payment take?">
    Most payments complete within 2-5 minutes. You'll receive confirmation as soon as it's processed.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="What if I need help?">
    Contact support@shorpay.com with your transaction details, and we'll assist you promptly.
  </FAQItem>
  
  <FAQItem question="Can I get a refund?">
    Refund policies are set by the merchant. Contact them directly for refund requests.
  </FAQItem>
</FAQ>

## Tips for Smooth Payments

### Do's
✅ **Exact Amounts**: Pay the exact amount shown
✅ **Reference Code**: Always include in memo
✅ **Quick Payment**: Complete within 30 minutes
✅ **Save Receipts**: Keep email confirmations

### Don'ts
❌ **Round Numbers**: Don't round up or down
❌ **Skip Memo**: Always include reference
❌ **Multiple Payments**: Don't split payments
❌ **Share Links**: Links are unique to you

## Getting Help

### Before Contacting Support

Check these first:
1. Payment amount matches exactly
2. Reference code included in memo
3. Used correct recipient details
4. Waited 5-10 minutes for processing

### Contact Support

If you need help:
- **Email**: support@shorpay.com
- **Include**: Transaction details, screenshot
- **Response Time**: Usually within 24 hours

### Information to Provide

When contacting support, include:
- Payment link URL
- Transaction amount
- Payment method used
- Time of payment
- Any error messages
